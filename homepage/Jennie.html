Jennie Facts

Her early life and talents:

She was born on January 16, 1996, in Anyang, a city in the province of Gyeonggi, South Korea. Jennie lived in Auckland, New Zealand for 5 years.She trained for 5 years 11 months at YG entertainment. She is fluent in Korean, Japanese, and English. She has 2 puppies, Kai and Kuma. She doesn’t have any siblings.


Music Career

“SOLO” is the first song released for the solo projects of BLACKPINK.

Jennie released a song called "Solo" on November 12th, 2018. With this song, Jennie showed her fearless and independent side.Jennie said "SOLO is about the story of many girls who, rather than being dumped, think it should be the case that they be someone who they’re not to suit their partner. Because they want to be loved. I wanted to express the feelings of those girls. Even though you work so hard at it, you end up getting hurt and frustrated, so rather than keep up an appearance that isn’t yourself, go and find your own self. It’s a song that expresses freedom."

You can find listen to her song and music video: Solo.
